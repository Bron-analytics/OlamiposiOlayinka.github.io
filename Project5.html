Work Management Application For Kehinde Kassim & Co.

Project Summary

The Work Management Application for Kehinde Kassim & Co. project was initiated in January 2021 to improve the firm's work processes and enhance the quality of accounting and tax-related services provided to clients. The project involved developing a software tool that enables the firm to track and manage tasks, deadlines, and deliverables associated with different accounting and tax-related services provided to clients. The application was designed to prioritize and allocate resources, assign tasks, and monitor progress, ensuring that work is completed on time and within budget. The application also provides a centralized repository for all relevant documents and communications related to each client, improving collaboration and communication among team members.
